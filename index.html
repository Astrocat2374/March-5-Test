What are Wireframes?

Wireframes are a basic layout of your webpage. We use them because it is easier to make a change on paper than in code, and it's alos easier to visualize.

Git Workflow

When you collaborate, you are supposed to do what the team leader tells you to. You push up to your own branch, but it is up to the team leader whether or not he wants to merge it with the master branch.
